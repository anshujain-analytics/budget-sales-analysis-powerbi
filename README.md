# 💰 Budget & Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that compares actual sales performance against planned budgets. The dashboard helps management monitor sales trends, identify performance gaps, and evaluate category and product-level contributions to support strategic business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Organizations need to continuously compare actual sales with planned budgets to measure business performance and identify areas requiring attention. This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor budget achievement, analyze sales variance, and identify top and underperforming products.

## 📈 Key KPIs

- **Total Sales:** 3,404M
- **Total Budget:** 3,579.26M
- **Sales Variance:** -175.67M
- **Budget Achievement:** 95.09%

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- The organization achieved **95.09%** of its planned sales target.
- Total sales reached **3,404M**, resulting in a **negative variance of 175.67M** against the allocated budget.
- **Category 3** contributed the highest share of total sales (**35.7%**), making it the strongest performing category.
- **Product 7** generated the highest sales (**245.32M**), closely followed by Products 2 and 3.
- **Product 4** recorded the largest negative sales variance (**-55.87M**), indicating a significant performance gap compared to the budget.
- Quarterly analysis shows sales remained relatively consistent throughout the year but stayed below the planned budget in every quarter.

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

- Investigate the causes of the **175.67M sales shortfall** and implement corrective actions.
- Review the performance of Products **4, 5, and 2**, which contributed the largest negative budget variances.
- Replicate successful strategies used for **Product 7** across other product lines.
- Allocate additional marketing and sales efforts toward high-performing product categories.
- Monitor budget performance regularly using interactive dashboards to support proactive decision-making.

---

## 💼 Business Value

This dashboard enables management to monitor sales performance against budget, identify underperforming products, evaluate category contributions, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall business performance.
